Explore the World with Me!

Capturing memories one journey at a time.

Paris - The City of Lights

From the Eiffel Tower to charming cafés, every corner of Paris had a story to tell. I walked along the Seine and watched the sunset with a warm croissant in hand.

Mountains of Nepal

Challenging treks, breathtaking views, and spiritual serenity—Nepal was a life-changing experience. The Himalayas made me feel both tiny and infinite.

Tokyo - Future Meets Tradition

Lights, sushi, and serenity. Tokyo was a perfect blend of modern life and traditional culture. I visited temples and shopped in Shibuya, all in a single day.
